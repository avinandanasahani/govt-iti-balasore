Our Specialized ITI Trades

Govt. ITI Balasore offers diverse technical programs designed to provide hands-on practical experience, state-of-the-art training, and direct industry placement. Explore our range of Engineering and Non-Engineering trades that empower students for a successful career.

COPA

High demand IT program focusing expertise advanced VBA Excel, fundamentals, network setups, intensive training web development technologies (HTML5, CSS3, JS basics). Includes introductions database management (MySQL) accounting software like Tally indispensable career paths corporate.

Draughtsman (Mechanical)

Create highly detailed engineering drawings, blueprints, and complex 3D models. Master international standard software like AutoCAD and basic solid modeling, translating design concepts into precise technical manufacturing plans. Fundamental for career paths in R&D departments and manufacturing units.

Electrician

Comprehensive Lifecycle training of installation, maintenance, and repair of wiring, machinery, and control panels. Focus on interpreting circuit diagrams, mastering domestic and industrial high-voltage systems, utilizing advanced testing instruments like multimeters for safe grounding.

Electronics Mechanic

Dives deep into electronics circuits, microprocessors, and advanced communication systems. Expert skills in PCB designing, precise soldering, and testing and repairing complex digital devices like medical equipment and modern automated industrial electronics.

Fitter

Master the structural framework of the manufacturing world. extensive hands-on practice involving high-precision filling, drilling, tapping, and metal-cutting operations. Trainees learn to read complex engineering blueprints to fabricate machinery components.

Machinist

Students become expert operators of heavy-duty machinery like lathes, milling machines, shapers, and grinders. A core focus on shaping metal parts with extreme precision, achieving micron-level fits and tolerances fundamental for component manufacturing and defense sectors.

Mechanic Motor Vehicle

This comprehensive program provides practical skills in repairing, overhauling, and maintaining motor vehicle lifecycles of both petrol and diesel engines. trainees gain hands-on with automotive transmission, suspension, breaking, and utilization modern computerized vehicle diagnostics.

Mechanic Electric Vehicle

A futuristic trade targeting the booming Electric Vehicle industry. Trainees become specialists in lithium-ion battery management, Battery Management Systems (BMS), and advanced electric powertrains. Essential focus on high-voltage safety, regenerative braking, and EV controller optimization.

ICTSM

Equips students with specialized skills to troubleshoot modern enterprise-level IT hardware and diverse server infrastructures. Extensive training covers computer assembly, OS installation (Windows/Linux), advanced software troubleshooting, detailed **LAN/WAN networking concepts, configuring routers**, and security management.

R&AC Technician

Trainees acquire practical skills in thermodynamics principles of the modern refrigeration lifecycle. Extensive advanced leak detection techniques and precise gas charging processes. A strong emphasis on servicing energy-efficient inverter technology, managing massive central AC plants and vital cold storage facilities.

Turner

A specialized machining trade entirely focused on mastering center lathe operations. Expert skills in complex metal cutting, taper turning, and eccentrical turning. extensive practice interpreting technical drawings for manufacturing precision shafts, pulleys required engineering and defense sectors.

Wireman

Specializing in wiring installations residential, commercial, and massive industrial complexes. extensive practical focus interpreting blueprint electrical layouts, safety grounding, efficient power distribution management. multiple wiring systems, advanced distribution board configurations indispensable professional career paths construction.

Mechanic (Tractor)

Essential for agricultural sector heavy-duty technical training mainenance, dismantling, repairing diverse farming machinery. deep study transmission, hydraulic lift components, power take-off (PTO) units, heavy diesel engines, preparing technical roles manufacturing units agriculture collectives.

Mechanic Diesel

Master the intricate Lifecycle high-performance diesel engines. detailed complex fuel injection pumps (FIP), Common Rail Direct Injection (CRDI) systems troubleshoot hands-on experience large diesel engines transport buses locomotives defense earth-moving industries.

Plastic Processing Operator

Unique core mechanical trade deeper understanding polymer science properties, expertise running automatic injection molding blow molding, extrusion manufacturing lifecycle. core focus detailed cycle optimization, graduates essential career paths diverse automobile component massive toy manufacturing.

Solar Technician

Futuristic green-energy technical program extensively focus highly-demand renewable technologies complete PV system designing, panel mounting structures off-grid wiring safety essential diagnosing solar plant performance thriving career paths booming rooftop organizations solar organizations.

Plumber

Dives deep into mastering water supply networks, intricate drainage, modern sanitary fittings installation domestic industrial Lifecycle comprehensive practical diverse pipe joint techniques, understanding blueprint reading blueprint layouts crucial real estate construction firefighting.

Welder

A foundational Engineering fabrication trade, MASTERING technical metal fusing technical using ARC, GAS, advanced MIG/MAG, TIG welding comprehensive practical techniques required across countless Lifecycle extensive positions graduates non-destructive technical testing Indispensable ship-building massive defense organizations.
