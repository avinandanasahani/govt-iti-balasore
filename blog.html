Computer Operator and Programming Assistant (COPA)

The COPA trade is the gateway to the digital and corporate world. It trains students in computer hardware, software installation, basic networking, web development (HTML/CSS/JS), and database management.

Career Opportunities & Job Fields

  • Corporate Sector: Junior Web Developer, Data Entry Operator, IT Support Assistant, and Database Administrator in MNCs and tech startups.
  • Government Sector: High demand in DRDO, ISRO, and Railways for Office Assistants, Computer Operators, and Data Processing roles.
  • Self-Employment: Freelance web developer, opening a cyber cafe, or setting up a computer repair and networking shop.

NCVT Exam Types

  • Trade Theory (CBT): Objective questions on computer fundamentals, MS Office, programming basics, and networking.
  • Employability Skills (CBT): English literacy, IT literacy, and communication skills.
  • Trade Practical: Hands-on exam involving tasks like writing HTML codes, applying advanced Excel formulas, or configuring a local network.
Exam Preparation Strategy: Don't just memorize theory! Practice writing HTML/JS code and MS Excel VBA macros daily on your computer. For the CBT exam, solve the NIMI mock tests regularly, as most theory questions are directly picked from the official Bharat Skills question bank.

Fitter

A core mechanical trade, Fitter forms the backbone of the manufacturing industry. Trainees learn to assemble, fabricate, and maintain heavy machinery components using precision measurement and cutting tools.

Career Opportunities & Job Fields

  • Heavy Engineering & Defense: Essential roles in Ordnance Factories, HAL, BHEL, and shipyards as structural fitters and machine assemblers.
  • Railways & Auto Sector: Massive recruitment for Assistant Loco Pilots (ALP), Railway Technicians, and assembly line workers in companies like Tata Motors and Maruti Suzuki.
  • Oil & Gas: Pipe fitters and maintenance technicians in refineries like IOCL and ONGC.

NCVT Exam Types

  • Trade Theory & WCS (CBT): MCQs on metals, limits/fits/tolerances, cutting tools, and Workshop Calculation & Science.
  • Engineering Drawing (Formative/Practical): Blueprint reading and drawing mechanical components.
  • Trade Practical: Extremely crucial. You will be given a raw metal block and asked to create a specific male-female joint using hand tools within a time limit.
Exam Preparation Strategy: For practicals, master your filing techniques to achieve the perfect "micron-level" finish. For CBT, focus heavily on Workshop Calculation and specific measurement readings (Vernier Caliper, Micrometer). Study the NIMI syllabus thoroughly.

Electrician

One of the most evergreen and highly demanded trades. Electrician training covers the installation, maintenance, and repair of electrical wiring, industrial motors, transformers, and control panels.

Career Opportunities & Job Fields

  • Power Grids & Boards: Junior Lineman and Sub-station Attendants in State Electricity Boards (like Tata Power, NTPC).
  • Construction & Infrastructure: Industrial wiring experts for real estate projects, metro rail corporations, and massive manufacturing plants.
  • Self-Employment: Government-certified electrical contractors for domestic and commercial electrical setups.

NCVT Exam Types

  • Trade Theory (CBT): Questions on Ohm’s law, AC/DC circuits, transformer mechanics, and electrical safety standards.
  • Trade Practical: Real-world tasks such as winding a motor, setting up a 3-phase connection, stair-case wiring, and using multimeters.
Exam Preparation Strategy: Safety first! Always draw circuit diagrams before practical exams. For the CBT, memorize the standard symbols of electrical components, formulas for current/voltage calculation, and strictly follow the Bharat Skills question bank.

Machinist

Machinists are the experts who shape metal blocks into highly precise functional parts using heavy-duty machinery like lathes, milling machines, shapers, and grinders.

Career Opportunities & Job Fields

  • Automobile Components: Operating CNC and conventional machines to manufacture gears, shafts, and engine parts for auto-giants.
  • Aerospace & Defense: Precision machining for aircraft components in HAL and defense sector ordnance factories.
  • Tool & Die Making: Specialized jobs in tool room industries manufacturing plastic injection molds and metal stamping dies.

NCVT Exam Types

  • Trade Theory (CBT): Questions on cutting speeds, feed rates, CNC programming basics, and machine tool anatomy.
  • Trade Practical: Operating a lathe or milling machine to cut a metal job (like gear cutting or step turning) precisely to the dimensions given in the engineering drawing.
Exam Preparation Strategy: Practical perfection is key here. Understand how to calculate spindle speed and feed rate before standing at the machine. For CBT, study the properties of different cutting tool materials (like Carbide, HSS) from NIMI books.

Electronics Mechanic

This high-tech trade focuses on the microscopic world of electronic circuits, microprocessors, logic gates, and communication systems. Trainees learn to test, repair, and assemble modern electronic devices.

Career Opportunities & Job Fields

  • Consumer Electronics: Service engineers and assembly technicians for smartphone, TV, and home appliance manufacturing units (Samsung, LG, Foxconn).
  • Telecom Sector: Network maintenance and hardware troubleshooting for telecom giants like Jio and Airtel.
  • Medical Electronics: Highly paid roles maintaining complex electronic equipment in large hospitals and research labs.

NCVT Exam Types

  • Trade Theory (CBT): Semiconductors, diodes, logic circuits, microcontrollers, and PCB designing concepts.
  • Trade Practical: Fault finding in circuits using an Oscilloscope (CRO), precise micro-soldering, and assembling a working electronic project.
Exam Preparation Strategy: Clear your basics of digital electronics (AND, OR, NOT gates). Practice reading the color codes of resistors instantly. For practicals, keep your hands steady during soldering and ensure you know how to use a digital multimeter perfectly.

Mechanic Motor Vehicle (MMV)

Comprehensive training in the repair, overhauling, and maintenance of light and heavy motor vehicles. Covers engine lifecycles, transmission systems, steering, and modern computerized vehicle diagnostics.

Career Opportunities & Job Fields

  • Automobile Service Stations: Head mechanics and diagnostic specialists in authorized service centers of Hyundai, Mahindra, and Toyota.
  • State Transport Corporations: Fleet maintenance engineers for state buses and logistics companies.
  • Manufacturing Plants: Quality check and assembly line inspectors in the fast-growing automotive manufacturing sector.

NCVT Exam Types

  • Trade Theory (CBT): 2-stroke/4-stroke engine mechanics, BS-VI emission norms, suspension systems, and vehicle aerodynamics.
  • Trade Practical: Dismantling and assembling engine components, bleeding brakes, and using OBD-II scanners to find electronic sensor faults.
Exam Preparation Strategy: Theory questions often focus on modern tech like CRDI and MPFI systems, so don't just study old engine models. For practicals, knowing the exact names and sizes of spanners and tools used for specific engine parts will get you full marks.

Draughtsman (Mechanical)

The architects of the mechanical world. This trade trains students to create highly detailed engineering drawings, blueprints, and 3D models using CAD software to translate ideas into manufacturing plans.

Career Opportunities & Job Fields

  • R&D Departments: Working with design engineers in Research and Development centers to create 3D prototypes.
  • Heavy Fabrication: Creating structural blueprints for shipbuilding, pipeline layouts, and large-scale manufacturing units.
  • Freelance Design: Providing AutoCAD and SolidWorks 3D modeling services to global clients online.

NCVT Exam Types

  • Trade Theory (CBT): Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing (GD&T), projection types (1st angle vs 3rd angle), and drafting standards.
  • Trade Practical (Software): You will be given a complex physical object or a 2D sketch and asked to create a perfect 3D model and its orthographic projections using AutoCAD.
Exam Preparation Strategy: Speed on the software is critical. Memorize all major AutoCAD keyboard shortcuts. Ensure you strictly follow dimensioning rules and line types (hidden lines, center lines) as per BIS standards during your practical exam.

Mechanic Electric Vehicle

The future of the auto industry. Trainees learn lithium-ion battery management, EV powertrains, and high-voltage safety protocols.

Career Opportunities

  • EV Manufacturers: High demand in companies like Tata Motors, Ather, and Ola Electric.
  • Battery Tech: Roles in Battery Management System (BMS) testing and EV charging station maintenance.
Exam Strategy: Focus completely on electric motor principles and battery chemistry for CBT. Safety precautions are heavily tested.

Information Communication Technology System Maintenance

Equips students to troubleshoot enterprise-level IT hardware, server infrastructures, and complex LAN/WAN networks.

Career Opportunities

  • IT Parks & BPOs: System administrators, network technicians, and IT hardware support.
  • Telecom: Hardware maintenance in BSNL, Jio, and broadband service providers.
Exam Strategy: Practice crimping LAN cables and configuring routers for practicals. Memorize OSI model layers for CBT.

Refrigeration & Air Conditioning Technician

Master the thermodynamics of cooling systems. Covers domestic ACs, commercial cold storage, and advanced inverter tech.

Career Opportunities

  • Appliance Giants: Service engineers for Voltas, LG, and Godrej.
  • Commercial Sector: Managing central AC plants in malls, hospitals, and pharmaceutical cold storage.
Exam Strategy: Perfect your gas charging and leak detection techniques. Study the refrigeration cycle diagram thoroughly for CBT.

Turner

Specialized machining focused on center lathe operations, taper turning, and precision metal shaping for manufacturing.

Career Opportunities

  • Defense & Railways: Manufacturing precision shafts and pulleys.
  • Heavy Engineering: Operating CNC lathes in large-scale auto component factories.
Exam Strategy: Speed and accuracy on the Lathe machine dictate your practical marks. Understand thread cutting calculations.

Wireman

Specialists in complex wiring installations for residential, commercial, and industrial complexes, focusing on safety grounding.

Career Opportunities

  • Real Estate: Chief wiring contractors for new building projects.
  • Power Sector: Managing industrial distribution boards and 3-phase wiring networks.
Exam Strategy: Blueprint reading is crucial. Know your Indian Electricity (IE) rules inside out for the CBT exam.

Mechanic (Tractor)

Essential for the agricultural sector. Deep study of heavy diesel engines, hydraulic lifts, and Power Take-Off (PTO) units.

Career Opportunities

  • Agri-Machinery: Technicians in Mahindra, Sonalika, and John Deere service centers.
  • Government Sector: Roles in state agriculture departments and farming collectives.
Exam Strategy: Focus heavily on hydraulic systems and transmission mechanics for both theory and practicals.

Mechanic Diesel

Mastering the lifecycle of high-performance diesel engines, Fuel Injection Pumps (FIP), and CRDI systems.

Career Opportunities

  • Logistics & Transport: Fleet maintenance for state transport buses and trucking companies.
  • Railways & Marine: Operating and maintaining heavy diesel locomotive engines and generator sets.
Exam Strategy: Understand the differences between petrol and diesel engine cycles clearly. Practice injector calibration.

Plastic Processing Operator

A core mechanical trade covering polymer science, injection molding, blow molding, and extrusion manufacturing.

Career Opportunities

  • Manufacturing Sector: High demand in auto-parts, toy manufacturing, and PVC pipe production units.
Exam Strategy: Study polymer properties and heating cycle times. Practical exams test your ability to set up molds quickly.

Solar Technician (Electrical)

A futuristic green-energy program focusing on PV system designing, panel mounting, and off-grid/on-grid wiring.

Career Opportunities

  • Renewable Energy: Installation experts for rooftop solar companies and massive solar farms (Adani Solar, Tata Power).
Exam Strategy: Focus on battery storage calculation and inverter syncing. Memorize sunlight angle formulas for CBT.

Plumber

Mastering water supply networks, intricate drainage, sanitary fittings, and diverse pipe joint techniques.

Career Opportunities

  • Construction & Infra: Essential for real estate projects, city water supply boards, and firefighting pipeline setups.
Exam Strategy: Blueprint reading for water layouts is highly tested. Practice perfect threading and watertight PVC jointing.

Welder

A foundational fabrication trade mastering ARC, GAS, advanced MIG/MAG, and TIG welding techniques.

Career Opportunities

  • Heavy Industries: Indispensable in shipbuilding, defense manufacturing, and cross-country pipeline projects.
Exam Strategy: Your practical exam is all about the perfect weld bead and zero porosity. For CBT, study welding defects and safety gases.
